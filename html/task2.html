BURGER

Sink your teeth into a delicious restaurant-style, hamburger recipe. I like the hamburgers most because hamburger is a blank canvas for whatever you want to create. You can even make a vegetarian version if that works better with your diet. Type of protein, variety of toppings, different types of buns. It really is up to the chef to create exactly what he or she wants at the time.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground lean (7% fat) beef
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1/4 cup fine dried bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire
  • 1 or 2 cloves garlic, peeled and minced

Recipe

  1. In a bowl, mix ground beef, egg, onion, bread crumbs, Worcestershire, garlic, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper until well blended. 🥩
  2. Divide mixture into four equal portions and shape each into a patty about 4 inches wide. 🔪
  3. Lay burgers on an oiled barbecue.Cook burgers, turning once, until browned on both sides and no longer pink inside.🔥
  4. Lay buns, cut side down, on grill and cook until lightly toasted, 30 seconds to 1 minute. ⏲
  5. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on bun bottoms. Add lettuce, tomato, burger, onion, and salt and pepper to taste. Set bun tops in place.🍔
